# AI Evaluation Methodology

## Purpose

This document describes the methodology used throughout this repository to evaluate AI-generated responses.

The objective is to provide consistent, transparent, and reproducible evaluations across different domains.

---

# Evaluation Process

Every evaluation follows seven steps.

## Step 1

Read the user's prompt carefully.

Understand exactly what is being requested.

---

## Step 2

Read the AI response.

Do not judge immediately.

---

## Step 3

Identify errors.

Possible errors include:

- Factual errors
- Logical errors
- Grammar mistakes
- Missing information
- Misleading statements
- Safety concerns

---

## Step 4

Evaluate quality.

Each response is analyzed according to five dimensions.

- Accuracy
- Completeness
- Clarity
- Helpfulness
- Safety

---

## Step 5

Write a detailed explanation.

Explain:

- what is wrong
- why it is wrong
- how it can be improved

---

## Step 6

Rewrite the response.

The improved version should be:

- accurate
- complete
- easy to understand
- technically correct

---

## Step 7

Assign confidence.

Estimate how confident you are in your evaluation.
